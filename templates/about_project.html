Welcome to HealthMsgU

This is a website for researchers to gauge public perception and find trends regarding health topics. Posts are first gathered from Twitter, and then analyzed to generate visualized statistical data.


How to use the site

  • Enter search terms on the Home page.
  • After your query has been entered, you can click the results button in the navigation bar.
  • Choose how to visualize your data from 3 options: Maps, Word Frequency and Word cloud.


FAQs

Q. How to contact the creators?
A. To contact the creators click on contact us for more information.

Q. When did this project start?
A. The project started in Fall 2017.

Q. How did this project come to be?
A. This project began as a way to get information to researchers about the way the public views the medication for certain diseases. Thus, from this data, ways to better inform the public could be made.
